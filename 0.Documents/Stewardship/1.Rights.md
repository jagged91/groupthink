# Stewardship - Rights
### Groupthink - Version 0.6

---

## What This Is

These are the rights that apply when a group stewards shared resources. They build on the Core rights (Documents/Core/1.Rights.md) and extend them.

---

## Rights of Stakeholders

### 1. Standing through dependence

Everyone who depends on a resource is a stakeholder in its commons. If you drink from a river, you are a stakeholder of that river's commons. This standing is inherent and exists independently of any group's recognition.

### 2. The right to invoke

When any decision at any level touches a shared resource, any stakeholder - member or not, invited or not - can say: "This exceeds what the commons can sustain." An invocation must point to specific, observable evidence. The decision-makers must examine the evidence before proceeding. The invocation and the response are recorded.

### 3. The right to knowledge

Determining what the commons can sustain is ongoing. Understanding its capacity requires observation, measurement, experience, and expertise. This understanding is never final and belongs to everyone - anyone can contribute knowledge, and the knowledge must be shared.

### 4. The right to sustainable use

No decision, at any level, can legitimately exceed what the commons can sustain. This is a recognition of physical reality.

### 5. Nesting

Each commons is local and specific - the smallest area where a resource can be meaningfully understood by the people who depend on it. When one commons' decisions affect another, the affected stakeholders coordinate. Coordination between commons follows the same principles as coordination between groups.

---

## Rights of the Commons Itself

### 6. Ecosystems have structural standing

The living systems that sustain the commons have structural standing. Their needs are constraints on use.

### 7. Future stakeholders have standing

People who will depend on a resource in the future hold a stake in decisions made now. Those who can speak are responsible for considering those who cannot.

---

*This is the rights document for Steward. These rights apply in addition to the Core rights whenever a group manages shared resources.*
